How our tuition rates work

Depending on what age your child is the price will be slightly different. Different kids need different things, as such younger kids like infants will take a bit more to take care of. Please comply with our rules before submitting an application as prices should be paid in full by the end of the week.

Young Toddler

Young Toddlers will be a bit more expensive to take care of as they are very sensitive. Diapers, bottles and wipes will be bought and the baby will be seen to. Not to worry as each baby will be assigned their own bottle and cot.

Older Toddlers

Older Toddlers will be a bit easier, but can still be a bit difficult to take care of. They will need bottles and from time to time diapers too.

Preschool

Preschool children will be tended to with a range of activities, as this is the time where they start being the most active. Crayons, colouring books, toys will be provided.

School age

School age (or round about 6-8 year olds) will be the most playful of them all. Toys, books and a range of activities will be provided.
